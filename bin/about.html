"It's the end of history. Sea levels are rising at an alarming rate. The protagonists 'I', 'you' and 'Matthew' have used the proceeds of their 'Life boat Empire' to seclude themselves in the luxury of a seventieth floor apartment"
"Confetti", also titled "Late Spring", is a sprawling, unfinished, unfinishable novel, being given away for free in no particular. It is a novel set in a future much like our present, only slightly shittier and mostly submerged beneath the sea.
